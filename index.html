Avand’s Front-end Web Development

Hey there! You’ve just stumbled upon Avand’s front-end web development curriculum designed for use at General Assembly in San Francisco.

More information about the course is available at General Assembly. And more information about Avand is available on his website.

Final Projects

Everyone always wants to know, "what will I build?" Good question. Previous students have built all kinds of projects: games, quizzes, portfolio websites, search engines, and maps. You'll find examples here:

  • Section 37 Final Projects: Scroll down and follow the links under the "Final Project" column on the right for each student.
  • Avand's compilation: Sadly, many of these projects have collected dust over the years and may no longer function but a few still work. It should give you an idea of the type of work you can do in this course.
  • GA's Gallery: GA has been collecting the "best-of" final projects from all the FEWD instances.
